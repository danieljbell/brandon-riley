About Brandon

Brandon Riley (that’s me… because I don’t make enough moolah to pay some poor schmuck to write witty and complimentary characteristics or anecdotes) is a father, teacher, supervisor, musician, and aspiring entertainer. (Continuity=Third Person) He has been playing guitar, singing, and writing music since the age of 13. He has been involved in a number of ensembles and musical projects throughout the years, but is currently focusing on his academic development and career. He has a bachelor’s in German and a master’s in TESOL from Arkansas Tech University, where he taught English for two years and has acquired his teaching licensure. Music will, however, always be a prominent influence and passion in his life, propelling him to strive to be as polished and accessible as possible.

Brandon is available for beginner and intermediate guitar instruction, private parties, and weddings. If interested, please leave a comment below or contact 479-857-sixtyeightzeroseven. Enjoy the videos!
